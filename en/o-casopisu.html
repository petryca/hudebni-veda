About the journal

GENERAL INFORMATION

The Institute of Art History of the Czech Academy of Sciences has been the publisher of the peer reviewed journal Hudební věda since 2019. Linking up with the tradiiton established by the research paper collections Musikologie and Hudební věda, this journal has ranked from its establishment in 1964 among the standard specialist periodicals in the field of musicology.

The journal's aim is to publish scholarly articles, reviews, conference/symposium reports and documents related to musical culture, with an emphasis on specific Czech aspects of current pursuits in musicology and music theory.

This journal is a fully peer reviewed periodical, with published texts subjected to the standard double-blinded reviewing process.

Published by the Institute of Art History of the Czech Academy of Sciences

  • appearing four times a year
  • ISSN 0018-7003 (Print)
  • ISSN 2694-6998 (Online)
  • Evidenční číslo MK ČR: E 4890

The Hudební věda / Musicology is included or indexed in the following indexes and databases: Central European Journal of Social Sciences and Humanities (CEJSH), International Index to Music Periodicals, Music Index, RILM Abstracts of Music Literature, SCOPUS and Web of Science (WOS).

Digital access and archiving policy

Full texts of articles are available in the Digital Library CAS, which is the primary source and repository of academic publications at the Czech Academy of Sciences.
